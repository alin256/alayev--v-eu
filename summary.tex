\ecvitem{\highlight{Personal description}}{
Sergey Alyaev grew up in Saratov (Russia) where he finished diploma in mechanics at the Saratov State University. He moved to Bergen in 2008 to pursue education in applied mathematics and completed MSc and PhD degrees at University of Bergen. Currently Sergey works as a researcher at IRIS where he applies mathematical modelling to solve problems of drilling, well modelling and geosteering. His main research interests include fluid-flow modelling, multi-scale methods, inverse problems and optimization.
